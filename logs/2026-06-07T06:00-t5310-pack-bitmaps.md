## Why not attempt a partial writer this round

A correct minimal writer must reproduce `num_selected_commits=106` /
`num_maximal_commits=107` exactly (selection thresholds + maximal walk) and emit
valid EWAH commit/type/xor chunks; a wrong or partial `.bitmap` would be loaded by
the existing `rev-list --use-bitmap-index` OID-only output path (added in commit
3941594) and the 16 currently-passing bitmap-dependent subtests here, plus the
green t7700/t5300/t5316/t5332, risk regression. With several agents sharing this
checkout, shipping a half-written container format is higher-risk than the
remaining failures. This is the dedicated multi-day pack-bitmap reader/writer
feature flagged in all 8 prior sessions; left OPEN.
